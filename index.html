About

Eyma Garçon is a Haitian filmmaker based in Vancouver, working across directing, writing, cinematography, and editing. His work is visually bold, emotionally grounded, and driven by character-first storytelling.

Eyma's practice centers on intimate, character-led narratives that sit between realism and stylized visual language. His approach emphasizes authentic human experience and explores themes of identity, belonging, and transformation. Whether directing, writing, or capturing images, he prioritizes collaboration and creating spaces where cast and crew can bring their full selves to the work.
